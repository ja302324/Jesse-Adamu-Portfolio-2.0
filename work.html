FAQ

1 WHAT KIND OF WORK DO YOU SPECIALIZE IN?
+
I focus on front-end web development, UI/UX design, and graphic design. I enjoy building clean, responsive interfaces and creating visuals that strengthen a brand’s identity.
2 ARE YOU CURRENTLY LOOKING FOR FULL-TIME OPPORTUNITIES?
+
Yes, I am graduating soon and actively exploring roles in front-end development, UI/UX design, and creative web design where I can contribute, learn, and grow with a team.
3 DO YOU TAKE ON FREELANCE PROJECTS AS WELL?
+
Yes, alongside job opportunities I accept selective freelance work, including websites, redesigns, branding materials, and UI/UX improvements.
4 WHAT TOOLS AND TECHNOLOGIES DO YOU WORK WITH?
+
For development I primarily use HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and modern frameworks. For design I work with Figma, Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and other industry tools.
5 WHAT DOES YOUR DESIGN PROCESS LOOK LIKE?
+
I start by understanding the goal and audience, then move into sketches or wireframes, prototypes, visual refinement, and finally translate the design into clean, functional code.
6 HOW LONG DOES A TYPICAL PROJECT TAKE?
+
Timelines depend on scope. Smaller tasks may take a few days, while larger UI/UX or development projects take longer. I provide realistic estimates once I understand the requirements.
7 DO I NEED TO KNOW HOW TO CODE TO WORK WITH YOU?
+
No, you do not need any coding knowledge. I handle the technical side and can also provide simple instructions if you want to update parts of your website yourself.
8 WHAT MAKES YOUR WORK DIFFERENT?
+
I bridge design and development, creating visual concepts and bringing them to life in code. My mix of self-taught graphic design and formal computer science training helps me balance creativity with structure.
9 HOW DO I GET STARTED WITH A PROJECT OR OPPORTUNITY?
+
You can reach out through my contact section or email. Whether you have a job opportunity, a collaboration idea, or a freelance project, I would be glad to hear about it.
