Approach

Colorful hospitality with structure

Visual direction

Champagne gold and citrus gradients paired with deep plum and turquoise to echo the multicultural menu. Rounded corners and grain overlays keep it warm and celebratory.

UX priorities

Fast access to hours, address, and phone; mobile-first menu sections; clear CTA to the live site; schema and meta tags aligned with local search.

Production

Vector logo package, print-ready PDFs for flyers/posters, apparel mockups, and a WordPress build with reusable blocks for quick specials updates.
