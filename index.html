About Me

I’m Jesse Adamu, a designer, storyteller, and strategic builder using visual creativity to help brands communicate with clarity and soul. With roots in Nigeria, shaped by global experience, and grounded in faith, I specialize in brand identity, web design, and digital strategy for small businesses, nonprofits, and purpose-driven projects. I co-founded Saved By Design, where I craft WordPress websites and cohesive branding systems that reflect each client’s unique mission. Whether I’m designing a logo, building a custom site, or mapping a full brand journey, I lead with intention, cultural insight, and a deep belief in excellence. My work blends clean design with strategic thinking, always aiming to create visuals that not only look good, but speak powerfully. Design is how I serve.
